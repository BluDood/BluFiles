# Danger Zone

From this page you will have access to some actions that can lead to data loss for your account.

> [!CAUTION]
> All actions described on this page are **_irreversible!_** Please make sure you understand the consequences of each action before proceeding.

## Delete Account

This will delete your account and all files, folders, pastes, collections, and shares associated with it.

## Revoke All Tokens

This will revoke all API tokens associated with your account. This can be useful if you think your account has been compromised.

## Delete All Files and Folders

This will delete all files and folders associated with your account. Shared file / folder links will also be invalid.

## Delete All Pastes

This will delete all pastes associated with your account. Shared paste links will also be invalid.

## Delete All Collections

This will delete all collections associated with your account. Shared collection links will also be invalid. The contained files will remain in your file manager.

## Revoke All Shares

This will invalidate all shared links associated with your account. The shared items will remain in your account, but the shared links will no longer work.
